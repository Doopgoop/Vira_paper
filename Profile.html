Our Story

VIRAPAPERS is the paper division of the well-established Viramsun Nigeria Limited. We are a leading paper conversion and distribution company in Nigeria, with a strong presence across West Africa since 1971.

Our journey began in the mid-1970s with paper trading, conversion, and the manufacturing of exercise books. Over the decades, we have evolved into a trusted supplier of a wide range of premium-quality paper and paper-related products sourced from Brazil, Europe, India, Indonesia, Singapore, and Thailand.

Today, VIRAPAPERS is recognized as a preferred distribution partner to multiple international paper mills.
