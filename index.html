Our skilled team combines expertise and innovation to deliver papers that inspire creativity and reliability in every sheet.

Our Production Methods

This production line integrates multiple operations into one continuous system, including reel unwinding with auto-splicing, flexographic printing, cross-cutting, overlapping and counting, cover inserting, wire stitching, folding, spine pressing, trimming, separating, and final stacking. All sections run in perfect synchronization through a central PLC control system, ensuring stable high-speed output and easy operation.

How the Process Works

  • Unwinding & Printing: Paper reels are unwound and automatically joined by the auto-splicer, ensuring continuous production. The web is then guided into the flexographic printing towers where ruling lines or custom designs are printed on both sides.
  • Cutting, Overlapping & Counting: The printed web is cut into sheets of preset length, overlapped, and transported to the collecting and counting unit. Here, accurate stacks are formed based on the required number of pages per book.
  • Cover Inserting & Book Formation: A pre-printed cover sheet is inserted onto each stack before it moves into the finishing stages. Each stack is wire-stitched along the spine, folded, spine-pressed, front-trimmed and side-trimmed, or separated into individual books before being neatly delivered to the stacking table.
